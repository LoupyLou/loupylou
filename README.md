## 👋 About me

I'm an engineering leader with 15+ years across software development, application security, and engineering management — currently serving as **Associate Director of R&D at StarCompliance**.

My background spans the full stack from hands-on `.NET` development through to leading multi-disciplinary engineering teams, designing secure-by-default architectures, and now integrating applied AI into R&D strategy.

I hold two Master's degrees — one in **Computing with Information Security** (The Open University) and one in **Computer Science with Artificial Intelligence** (University of Wolverhampton, in progress) — which gives me an unusual combination of deep security grounding and cutting-edge ML knowledge.

Outside of work I co-founded a coaching and mentoring company focused on engineering career growth, and I actively mentor engineers navigating the path from IC to leadership.
